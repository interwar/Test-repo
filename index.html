Sheila Wester-Swords


World Traveller


Philosopher and Poetess.



Sheila Wester Swords, A truly exemplary Aruban woman. While Aruba may "Doet" once a year, S.W.S. is year-long phenomenon, helping people and animals here on Aruba and far beyond our shores.

With her she poetry has enriched the lives of many. Her inciteful, far ranging and deeply piercing observations, she has redefined what it means to be an Aruban woman for a new generation of Aruban poets and explorers.

For the pride she daily injects back into the community, for her steadfast belief in leading by example, it gives us so much pride to be here today to honour this gifted lady with the highest award we can bestow upon her. S.W.S. the beloved Dame of Santa Cruz.
